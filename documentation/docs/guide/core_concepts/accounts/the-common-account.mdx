# The Common Account

The common account is controlled by the chain owner (defined in the chain root contract).

This account is used to store funds collected by fees, or sent to the chain L1 address, or to invalid contracts (this way the funds are not permanently lost).

## `harvest`

The `harvest` entry point allows the chain owner to move all funds available in the common account to their own L2 account.

### Parameters

- `ParamForceMinimumBaseTokens` - specify the amount of base tokens to leave in
  the common account (default: 3000)
